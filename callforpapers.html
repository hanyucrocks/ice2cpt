Call for Papers

The conference invites high-quality research contributions in all areas of Electrical, Electronics, and Computer Science. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • Smart Power Systems, Electric Vehicles, and Energy Management
  • Advanced Robotics, Autonomous Systems, and Control Technologies
  • Renewable Energy Systems, Solar Power, and Grid Integration
  • Wireless Communication, IoT, and Next-Generation Networks
  • Electromagnetic and Wireless Technologies
  • Cybersecurity, Blockchain, and Quantum Computing for Secure Systems
  • Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Computer Vision

Submit your paper by 30th June 2025.
